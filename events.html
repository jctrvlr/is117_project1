Events that changed the Internet

  1. 1950 - Origin of internet
  2. 1957 - Russia launches Sputnik, which surpasses the US in space tech
  3. 1958 - Bell Labs invents modem to convert digital signals to electrical signals
  4. 1962 - Licklider formulates 'Intergalactic Computer Network'
  5. 1965 - ARPA sponsors networkign studying to create first WAN
  6. 1972 - Fewer than 25 websites were online
  7. 1972 - Ray Tomlinson invents email program to send messages across distributed networks.
  8. 1974 - Beranek and Newman create Telenet which is the first commerical version of ARPAnet
  9. 1974 - The term "Internet" is used for the first time by Vint Cerf and Robert Kahn
  10. 1983 - Paul Mockapetris invents Domain Name System (DNS)
  11. 1983 - ARPAnet transitions to TCP/IP making it the first network to use it
  12. 1992 - Congress passes bill making the internet public
  13. 1995 - Bezos launches Amazon
  14. 1995 - Bill Gates releases Internet Explorer
  15. Present - Over 11 million domain names registered and nearly 70 million websites
